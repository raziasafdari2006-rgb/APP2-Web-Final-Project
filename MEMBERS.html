LISA

Lalisa Manobal, known as Lisa, is a Thai rapper, singer, dancer, and actress, who gained international fame as a member of the K-pop girl group BLACKPINK.

Early Life and Background

  • Birth: born on March 27, 1997, in Satuek, Buri Ram Province, Thailand. Her birth name was Pranpriya Manobal, which she later changed to Lalisa following a fortune-telling session, as Lalisa means one who is praised.

  • Family: She is an only child and has a supportive family background. Her mother, Chitthip Brüschweiler, and her stepfather, Marco Brüschweiler, a Swiss chef, played significant roles in her upbringing.

  • Education: Lisa moved to Bangkok at the age of three and attended Praphamontree II International School, where she developed her English language skills and passion for dance.

  • Career with BLACKPINK

  • Debut: Lisa debuted as a member of BLACKPINK under YG Entertainment in August 2016. She is recognized as the main dancer and lead rapper of the group, contributing significantly to their global success.

  • Achievements: BLACKPINK has become one of the best-selling girl groups of all time, with numerous hits and record-breaking music videos. Lisa's talent and charisma have made her a standout member of the group.

  • Solo Career

  • Solo Debut: In September 2021, Lisa released her debut single album "LALISA," which set several records, including the highest first-week sales for a female artist in South Korea. Notable Singles: The lead single "LALISA" and the viral track "Money" achieved significant milestones, with "Money" becoming the first song by a K-pop solo artist to reach one billion streams on Spotify.

  • Recent Work: In 2024, Lisa established her own management company, Lloud, and signed with RCA Records. She released her debut studio album "Alter Ego" in 2025, which debuted in the top ten of the US Billboard 200.

JISOO

JISOO, born Kim Ji-soo on January 3, 1995, is a South Korean singer, songwriter, and actress, best known as a member of the globally renowned girl group BLACKPINK.

Early Life and Background

  • Birth: Jisoo was born in Gunpo, Gyeonggi-do, South Korea. She has an older brother and sister. Education: She attended the School of Performing Arts Seoul and joined YG Entertainment as a trainee in 2011, training for five years before debuting.

  • Career with BLACKPINK

  • Debut: Jisoo debuted as a member of BLACKPINK on August 8, 2016, with the single album "Square One" under YG Entertainment.

  • Role: In BLACKPINK, Jisoo serves as the lead vocalist and visual, known for her powerful voice and stunning visuals. She is often referred to as the "mood maker" of the group due to her cheerful personality.

  • Solo Activities

  • Acting: Jisoo made her acting debut with a cameo in the 2015 series "The Producers" and later starred in the JTBC drama "Snowdrop" (2021-2022).

  • Solo Music: She released her debut single album "Me" on March 31, 2023, which became the best-selling album by a female soloist in South Korea. The lead single "Flower" achieved significant commercial success.

  • New Label: In 2024, Jisoo established her own label, Blissoo, and signed with Warner Records in 2025 for her solo activities.

  • Achievements

  • Awards: Throughout her career, Jisoo has received numerous accolades, including Golden Disc Awards and MAMA Awards. She is also the most-followed Korean actress on Instagram and has made a significant impact in the fashion industry as a global ambassador for Dior.

JENNIE Kim

Jennie Kim, known mononymously as Jennie, is a South Korean singer, rapper, and actress, recognized as a member of the globally successful girl group BLACKPINK and for her solo career.

Early Life and Background

  • Jennie: was born on January 16, 1996, in South Korea. She spent part of her childhood in New Zealand, where she studied at ACG Parnell College before returning to South Korea to pursue a career in music.

  • Training: She joined YG Entertainment in 2010, training for nearly six years before debuting with BLACKPINK in 2016.

  • Career with BLACKPINK

  • Debut: BLACKPINK debuted on August 8, 2016, and quickly rose to fame, becoming one of the best-selling girl groups of all time. Jennie serves as the main rapper and lead vocalist of the group.

  • Achievements: The group has broken numerous records, including being the first K-pop girl group to perform at Coachella in 2019.

  • Solo Career

  • Debut Single: Jennie launched her solo career with the single "Solo" in November 2018, which topped charts in South Korea and the US, and its music video became the first by a Korean female soloist to surpass one billion views on YouTube.

  • Recent Releases: In 2023, she released singles like "You & Me" and "One of the Girls," both achieving commercial success. Her debut studio album, "Ruby," released in 2025, sold over a million copies and included hits like "Mantra" and "Like Jennie".

ROSE

Rosé, born Roseanne Park, is a South Korean-New Zealand singer and songwriter, best known as the main vocalist and lead dancer of the globally renowned girl group BLACKPINK.

Early Life and Background

  • Debut: ROSé debuted with BLACKPINK on August 8, 2016, under YG Entertainment. The group quickly rose to fame, becoming one of the best-selling girl groups of all time.

  • Musical Contributions: As the main vocalist, Rosé has contributed to numerous hits, including "Ddu-Du Ddu-Du," "Kill This Love," and "How You Like That."

  • Solo Career

  • Solo Debut: Rosé launched her solo career with the single album "-R-" on March 12, 2021. The lead single, "On the Ground," achieved significant commercial success.

  • Recent Work: In 2024, she signed with The Black Label and Atlantic Records, releasing the hit single "APT." featuring Bruno Mars.

  • Achievements and Recognition

  • Awards: Rosé has received numerous accolades, including eight Guinness World Records, six MAMA Awards, and three Grammy nominations.

  • Influence: In 2025, she was named one of Time magazine's 100 Most Influential People and featured in Rolling Stone's list of living icons from Australia and New Zealand.

  • Personal Life

  • Interests: Rosé is known for her passion for fashion and serves as a global ambassador for brands like Yves Saint Laurent and Tiffany & Co.

  • Social Media: She is active on social media, connecting with fans globally.
